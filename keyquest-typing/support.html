Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your KeyQuest Typing account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to cs@uniqbuild.co.kr from your registered email address with the subject line "KeyQuest Typing Account Deletion Request".

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User profile information
  • Game progress data
  • All associated metadata

Data Retention

Some information may be retained for a limited period as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, including security, fraud prevention, and regulatory compliance.

Processing Time

Your account deletion request will be processed within 7 business days.

You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
